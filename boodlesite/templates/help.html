FAQ

Placing a Bid
  • To place a bid, go to the homepage to check all on-going auctions
  • Find the desired auction and click on the title to go to the auction title
  • At the auction, click on the "Place Bid" button near the latest bids
  • A pop-up will appear, enter the a desired bidding value in the input box
    *Please note: only bids higher than the current highest bid will go through
  • Once you place the desired bid, click submit and the bid should appear in latest bid
Creating a Store
  • Access your profile by clicking the link "My Profile" on the nav bar
  • Once accessed, below your profile picture is the button "Create Store"
  • Click this button and a pop up will appear where you input the desired store information
  • Once you click "Submit", the button will change into a link to access your store
Inventory Management
  • To access your store, click the "My Store" button on the profile page
  • At the store page, you can view the current items in your inventory
  • Each item has the option to edit and delete an item below the item name
  • To add an item, click the "Add Item" button at the bottom of the page
  • Once selected, you will be redirected to a page where you can input the necessary information for an item
  • Once filled, select "Add Item" button and the item should appear on your store page
Starting an Auction
  • To access your store, click the "My Store" button on the profile page
  • To start an auction, select the "Start Auction" button at the bottom of the page
    *Note: You must have an existing item to start an auction and you cannot create duplicate auctions for a single item
  • Once selected, you will be redirected to a page where you can input the necessary information for the auction
  • Once filled, select "Start Auction" button and the auction should appear on the homepage
Profile Management
  • Access your profile by clicking the link "My Profile" on the nav bar
  • Below the profile picture, there will be a button "Edit Profile"
  • Clicking this button, the you will be redirected to change your username
  • After filling it up and selecting "Save Changes". The changes will appear on the profile.
